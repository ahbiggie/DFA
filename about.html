DA’WAH FRONTIERS ACADEMY OKPELLA, EDO STATE

OUR MISSION

Da’wah Frontiers academy, Alhamdulillah, is an Islamic-cum-modern Educational Centre, established on the 22nd of September 2014 to provide Muslim Children with excellent quality education and facilities for the overall development. They are taught moral values and various skills to face the dynamic challenges which the students can learn, understand and practice their ‘way of life’ – Islam, as they strive for others. It molds the students’ personality according to righteous Islamic tenets and inculcates in them the values and concepts of caring, sharing and being just for truth and universal brotherhood and peace to prevail.

The overall goal of the academy is to groom the future leaders of this great nation, build a generation that will appreciate the essence of existence, Islamically solid, intellectually well equip and morally balanced. Our intention is to broaden the students’ appreciation for and interest in Arabic and Islamic studies at the same time offer them a qualitative modern/western education that will enable them compete in the contemporary challenges of the Industrial world.

The academy at the moment is to provide Educational opportunities to all Nigerians under the ages of nursery, primary, and secondary schools. In the nearest future, the Academy is targeted to grow above the secondary level. In shaa Allah.

Admission

Admission into the academy is open to all students who fulfill the admission requirements of the school and agree to abide by the code of conduct adopted by the academy.

vision

In summary, the general aims and objectives of the academy is among other things; -

  • To provide sound/Qualitative Islamic and modern education for Nigerian youths;
  • To establish a morally balanced education.
  • To prepare a generation that is God fearing, morally upright, intellectuals that will be able to defend themselves both in the theoretical and practical aspect of their chosen careers;
  • To support the economically and physically challenged;
  • Bridged the gap that exists between religious’ and mundane worldly studies;
  • Help in the education of the orphans and the poor; free of charge.
  • To engage the student in extracurricular and vocational activities that will help them be self-independent in the future.
  • Help in the transition to universities, polytechnics, colleges and other vocational institution after their secondary education via necessary assistance

Our Services

Tahfeez

In order to make the students commit the entire Qur’an into memory before they graduate from secondary school, we ensure that the first 1 hour (8 – 9am) every day is for Hifz (memorization of the Qu’ran). Also,a special regular Hifz programme to memorize the complete Qu’ran [Nursery, primary and secondary school students] has be initiated to ensure that they memorize the entire Qu’ran in not more than three (3) years.

The school aims at upbringing students, personifying the profound spirit, vision and skills that Muslims must have to be ‘Generation Next’ leaders and simultaneously be the torch bearers guiding the present world from darkness to light.

Parents Participations

The parents’ participation in their child’s/children’s development at school and home are invariable interlinked and vital for overall growth and stability of the child at home and school. The parents are therefore encouraged to meet, interact and review their children’s education and development with his/her teachers. They are also invited to join in the special events and programmes of the school like ‘Annual Day’, ‘Annual Sports’, Islamic Vision’, ‘personality Development for parents’, ‘student’s Public Talks’, ’exhibitions’. Conferences’ walimah Tul – Qur’an’, quranic competitions and others. The PTA meeting are compulsory at DFA. Influences of the society upon a child’s development are analysed critically in most of DFA PTA MEETINGS. An Un-Islamic environment can result in the corruption of a virtuous Muslim’s Islamic understanding, upbringing and values. These DFA parents realize that truly concerned Muslim parents with Taqwa (piety) and concern, educate their children in an Islamic school to avoid their children falling prey to the bad influences and immorality prevalent in society.

Islamic Ambience

Islamic ethos, etiquettes and behavior as per the Qur’an and authentic Sunnah Guidelines are encouraged in students at Da’wah Frontiers academy.

The DFA school uniform adheres to the Islamic dress code and Hijab. Congregational Prayers are offered daily in the school by students. The true understanding, motivation about and practice of Islamic guidelines are inculcated in the students by well versed and ‘modern Youth’ oriented teachers. The Character, Courage and confidence of the students are strengthened in their personality trait, coaxingly.

Educational Excursion Trips

To learn about ‘the world around us’, students are taken on Termly educational excursion to places of public or social importance to see and learn about that place and activities. As the students interact with different kinds of people and authorities as well as see different places and activities, their confidence, clarity and understanding of reality of life is enhanced. Apart from gaining knowledge by practically visiting important locations, the students also enjoy these recreations.

VOCATIONAL STUDIES, SKILLS ACQUISITION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

At DFA, Our students are exposed to skills acquisitions programes, the programme is built into the school’s curriculum and has a space in the timetable. Vocational training such as shoe making, bag making, beads and many other creativities are taught to the students by experts

Boarding

DAWAH FRONTIERS ACADEMY comprises of day and boarding session, the boarding session is open to both male and females students from age seven (7) above. The boarding students has the opportunity to learn more about the Deen, fast memorization of the Quran, learn morals, vocational activities while ensuring that their academic performances are not compromised

The Orphans and Less-Privileged at DFA-INTERNATIONAL, the education of orphans and less privileged is free. Our philosophy is that no orphan or less privileged should drop out of school before him/her last her parents or come from a less privileged home. The orphans and less privileged are awarded scholarship to study free (free tuition, books, transportations or accommodation and feeding for those who are willing to join the DFA boarding facilities) FROM THE AMIR’S DESK
